About Rob

Hi there, I'm Rob, a Yorkshire born Self-Published Author, Graphic Design Artist and Software Developer.

  • He Writes

    I completed a Masters Degree in Creative Writing in 2013 at the University of Salford, and have since gone on to publish 10 novels, with another 4 in development, all of which review 5-star reviews on Amazon. When I'm not using my talents to tell tales, I've put my creative side towards adverts, press releases, brochures, and much more.

    View literary work here.

  • He Designs

    I've been designing since I could hold a pencil. While other kids were out playing football, I was teaching myself Adobe Photoshop. Everything I know about designing, I've taught myself and the proof is in the work. My customers always love the work I create for them.

    See what people think of my work here.

  • He Markets

    In 2016, I completed a Diploma in Digital Marketing. I've worked with musicians, Youtubers, and companies big and small, creating marketing strategies, PR opportunities, promotional material, and much more.

    See some of my clients here.

  • He Codes

    In 2021, I completed a Diploma in Software Development, gaining knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and Django. I built this website, in case you wanted proof. I'm available to work on any development projects you may have, so if you're interested, please get in touch!

    View my Github here.

This combination of skills has made me an invaluable asset to any business looking for someone who can carry their vision.
