About Rob

Hi there, I'm Rob, a Yorkshire born Self-Published Author, Graphic Design Artist and Software Developer. But that only scratches the surface of my life, so I've put together a nifty timeline that showcases some of the highlights of my career.

  • Diploma in Software Development

    In 2021, I completed a Diploma in Software Development with the Code Institute. During this time, I expanded my knowledge in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript Python, MongoDB, Django, Bootstrap, JQuery and MySQL.
    The course was marked across 4 projects: User Centric Frontend Development, Interactive Frontend Development, Backend Development and Full Stack Frameworks. This website formed my final project, in case you were curious. You can view all my work and projects on my GitHub page via the link below:


    Github

    2020 - 2021

  • The James Stone thriller series

    Throughout the year of 2020, I released 4 novels continuing the James Stone series: The Killing Business, The Glorious Dead, The Damned and The Buried, and Ricochet, bringing the total number of books I have self-published up to 10. For all these novels, I have done everything myself, including editing, cover design, marketing and promotion.
    You can find out more about these books on my author website.


    Author Website

    2015 - 2020

  • The Wilf Ward Family Trust

    Across 2019 and 2020, I worked as a Marketing Officer with Yorkshire-based organisation, The Wilf Ward Family Trust. During this time, I rebuilt their brand from the ground up, unifying their message across all of their offices and ventures. This required the recreation of effective brand guidelines with a greater focus on their Yorkshire roots, procedures for recruitment marketing and social media advertising, and much more.
    You can read more about my work with The Wilf Ward Family Trust via the link below.


    The Wilf Ward Family Trust

    2019 - 2020

  • Chameleon Music Marketing

    Working freelance with music marketing service, Chameleon Music Marketing, I worked with clients all across the music industry, handling a wide array of marketing, PR and social media tasks. Working with approximately five shows per week on a three month schedule, it was my job to liaise with the marketing managers at each venue to create tailored plans, as well as working with media contacts such as the BBC and ITV to arrange interviews and competitions.
    You can read more about Chameleon Music Marketing via the link below.


    Chameleon Music Marketing

    2017 - 2018

  • Diploma in Digital Marketing

    During my time at Yorkshire Coast Homes, I completed a CAM Diploma in Digital Marketing with the Home Learning College. This proved pivotal in my decision to move into a self-employed marketing and design role, during which I have worked with a wide array of clients, including an award-winning indie band, podcasters and comedians, healthcare providers and music marketing services.
    You can read more about some of my clients via the link below.


    Portfolio

    2015 - 2016

  • Coastcall Independent Living

    In 2013, I began work as an Admin Assistant Apprentice in the Coastcall department at Yorkshire Coast Homes. During this time I developed skills in finance software, database management, marketing, research and promotion. I went on to achieve full employment in 2014, and went on to redesign the Coastcall team's brand, creating posters and brochures, along with a company mascot. I was elected to plan and design the company website, which launched in 2017.


    Coastcall

    2013 - 2017

  • Masters Degree in Creative Writing

    In 2012, I began my studies for my Masters Degree in Creative Writing at the University of Salford. This course taught me to look critically at my work, and to give and receive constructive feedback to and from peers. I have found this to be invaluable not only in reflecting on and improving my performance, but also in being able to communicate effectively and supportively with others on matters that might potentially be taken as criticism.
    It was during this time that I came up with the idea of James Stone, which has since gone on to become a globe-trotting action thriller book series. You can read more about these books via my author website below.
